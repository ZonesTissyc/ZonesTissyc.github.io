识别题目

求下列函数在给定点处的左右极限，并说明在定点处是否有极限. (1)$f(x)=1+\frac {|x+1|}{x+1}$，在$x=-1$处； (2)$f(x)=\lim\limits _{x→0}(\frac {2+e^{\frac {1}{x}}}{1+e^{\frac {4}{x}}}+\frac {sinx}{|x|})$，在$x=0$处

解答

(1)$\lim\limits _{x→-1^{+}}f(x)=2,\lim\limits _{x→-1^{-}}f(x)=0,\because \lim\limits _{x→-1^{+}}f(x)≠\lim\limits _{x→-1^{+}}f(x),\therefore \lim\limits _{x→-1^{-}}f(x)7$存在.

(2)$\lim\limits _{x→0^{+}}(\frac {2+e^{\frac {1}{x}}}{1+e^{\frac {4}{x}}}+\frac {sinx}{|x|})=\lim\limits _{x→0^{+}}(\frac {2e^{-\frac {4}{x}}+e^{-\frac {3}{x}}}{e^{-\frac {4}{x}}+1}+\frac {sinx}{x})=1$,

$\lim\limits _{x→0^{-}}(\frac {2+e^{\frac {1}{x}}}{1+e^{\frac {4}{x}}}+\frac {sinx}{|x|})=\lim\limits _{x→0^{-}}(\frac {2+e^{\frac {1}{x}}}{1+e^{\frac {4}{x}}}-\frac {sinx}{x})=1,\therefore \lim\limits _{x→0}f(x)=1$.
